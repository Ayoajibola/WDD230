Site Name

The name of this site is TEAM WEATHER RESOURCE HOME. As the name suggests, it is a forum for friends and neighbours to collaborate. While weather informaion can be useful, collaborations to provide real time updates from individuals will contribute to the accuracy of the weather data. This in turn will contribute to better decisions on social and economical activities.

Purpose

This web site will serve as an online community of people who share ideas and reports to improve the reliability of weather reports. This will enable them to support one another in maximising weather situations to improve thei lives.

While weather reporting is integral to the purpose of this site, the project is not just to report weather, It is a platform to socialise and collabotate to improve lives.

Everyone could use someones help and everyone has something to offer. People support each other. People could share new ideas, post local news that could potentialy help someone else.
