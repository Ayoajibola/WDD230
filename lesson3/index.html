Site Name

The name of this site is TEAM WEATHER RESOURCE. As the name suggests, it is a forum for friends and neighbours to collaborate. While weather informaion can be useful, collaborations to provide real time updates from individuals will contribute to the accuracy of the weather data. This in turn will contribute to better decisions on social and economical activities.

Purpose

This web site will serve as an online community of people who share ideas and reports to improve the reliability of weather reports. This will enable them to support one another in maximising weather situations to improve thei lives.

While weather reporting is integral to the purpose of this site, the project is not just to report weather, It is a platform to socialise and collabotate to improve lives.

Everyone could use someones help and everyone has something to offer. People support each other. People could share new ideas, post local news that could potentialy help someone else.

Domain Name

weatherforlife.org is available at https://domain.google for $12/year.

We have made effort to ensure that the domain name has the key word weather in it. This will push to appear on weather search results.

Team Weather Resource Logo

The logo consists of sunlight and a cloud on blue water as background the combination will suggest to visitors that the web site is all about weather.
